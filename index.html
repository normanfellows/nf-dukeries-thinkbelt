Progressing Architecture

How little need be known before action is worthwhile.
— Cedric Price, 1972

The nf-dukeries-thinkbelt repository explores architecture as a progressing discipline.

Originating in the Dukeries Thinkbelt (DTb) study and Cedric Price's Potteries Thinkbelt proposal, the repository investigates how architectural ideas persist, evolve and generate new forms of enquiry across time.

Architecture is not understood as the production of completed objects, but as the continual development of transformational hypotheses through which social, educational and territorial change may be explored.
